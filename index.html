Jill-Jênn Vie (also in French)

Hi! I'm a postdoctoral researcher in RIKEN AIP @ Tokyo and in Kashima's Machine Learning Lab @ Kyoto University.

I also write free software™ for the French Ministry of Education: I design the adaptive tests of PIX, an open-source platform for certifying the digital competences of French citizens.

Also I enjoy playing the piano! Together with two talented singers, we perform anime and video game music in concerts.
