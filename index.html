Jill-Jênn Vie

Hi! I'm a postdoctoral researcher at the RIKEN Center for AIP (Advanced Intelligence Project) in Tokyo, Japan, under the supervision of Hisashi Kashima, professor at Kyoto University.

I also work for the French Ministry of Education on PIX, an open-source platform for certifying the digital competences of French citizens. I design their adaptive tests.

Also I enjoy playing the piano! Together with two talented singers, we perform anime and video game music at concerts (Ghibli movies, Final Fantasy).

Contact : vie@jill-jenn.net.

Achievements

✅ Coauthor of the Python package tryalgo: short implementations of classic algorithms (minimum spanning trees, Knuth-Morris-Pratt, hungarian algorithm, Fenwick tree, etc.)
✅ Cofounded a programming summer school for K-12 girls: Girls Can Code!
✅ Cofounded an open-source anime recommender system: Mangaki
✅ Directed a TV show about algorithms that take control of our lives: Blame the Algorithm
✅ Missed an appointment with the French Minister of Education because I had to teach.
